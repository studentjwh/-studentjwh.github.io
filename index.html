About the App

TikTok Insights for Learning is an innovative application designed to transform your TikTok activity into a powerful educational tool. By securely accessing your activity and comment history, profile data, and comprehensive analytics via the TikTok API, our app provides a clear, organized, and insightful view of your digital engagement. Visualize your data with intuitive dashboards, apply simple filters to pinpoint specific interactions, and gain valuable insights into your content consumption and creation patterns. Whether for personal reflection, academic study, or digital literacy, TikTok Insights for Learning empowers you to understand your online presence like never before.

Key Features

  • Activity & Comment History: Easily review your past interactions and engagement, providing a chronological overview of your TikTok journey.
  • Profile Data Access: Understand your profile's reach, follower growth, and content performance with detailed metrics.
  • Comprehensive Analytics: Dive deep into your performance metrics with visual breakdowns of views, likes, shares, and comments for each of your videos.
  • Intuitive Data Visualization: See your data presented in easy-to-understand charts, graphs, and dashboards, making complex information accessible at a glance.
  • Simple Filtering: Quickly sort and analyze data with user-friendly filters based on date, content type, interaction type, and more, allowing for targeted insights.

Why Use This App?

  • Educational Insights: Transform raw data into actionable knowledge about your digital habits and content effectiveness.
  • Personal Growth: Reflect on your online presence, identify trends in your engagement, and discover opportunities for improvement.
  • Digital Literacy: Enhance your understanding of social media dynamics, data privacy, and the impact of your online activities.

Product and Scope Explanation

Products:

  • Login Kit: "Used to authenticate users via TikTok OAuth for educational analysis of user data workflows (e.g., verifying identity before processing DSRs)."
  • Data Portability API: "Integrated to demonstrate GDPR/CCPA compliance by allowing users to request and download their TikTok data in JSON/CSV formats for academic study."
  • Webhooks: "Configured to receive real-time notifications (e.g., DSR status updates) for testing automated workflows."

Scopes:

  • user.info.basic: "Access basic user profile data (username, profile photo) to verify identity during DSR submissions."
  • video.publish (if applicable): "Not used in this educational project, as we focus on data portability, not content creation."
